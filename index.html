What is HaPlaylist?

HaPlaylist is a tag based playlist management tool for Android. With HaPlaylist, you can tag your music files with arbitrary boolean values, and then use logical expressions to select the music you want to listen to.

HaPlaylist is not a music player, but rather it dynamically creates M3U playlists on your Android phone, for use with your existing media player.
